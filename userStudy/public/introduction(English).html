Welcome to the Recommending Car System

In our research project, we aim to interact with you by asking questions to identify your favorite used car from our database. The system consists of 5 rounds, with each round corresponding to a different algorithm. In each round, you will answer a series of consecutive questions. For each question, two cars will be presented, and you need to choose the one you prefer. At the end of each round, a car will be recommended to you. Based on the recommended car and the number of questions you have answered, you need to evaluate the algorithms by assigning scores.

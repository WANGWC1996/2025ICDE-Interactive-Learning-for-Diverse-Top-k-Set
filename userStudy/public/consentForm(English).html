Consent Form

The objective of this study is to design an innovative database system capable of interacting with users to learn their preferences and subsequently retrieve tuples based on these learned preferences. You will be interacting with an interactive database system by answering a few easy questions for about 30-60 minutes. We expect we can evaluate the interactive database system based on your feedback.

The data collected during the study will not contain any identity information of the Participant and will be used solely to analyze the performance of the interactive database system. The data will not be used for any other purpose.

We want to assure you that any data collected during this study will remain strictly confidential. We will take all necessary steps to protect your personal information and ensure that it is not shared with any unauthorized individuals or organizations. We promise that we will not disclose any individual sensitive data that may be obtained through this study. However, we may disclose general statistics, such as the overall findings of the study, to third parties.

Your participation in this study is voluntary, and you have the right to decline to answer any question or to withdraw from the survey at any time without any penalty or negative consequences.
